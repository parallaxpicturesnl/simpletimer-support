Privacy Policy

Simple Timer and Countdown
Last updated: February 12, 2026

Overview

Simple Timer and Countdown ("the App") is developed by Yannick Verhoeven. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data (events, timers, templates, and settings) is stored locally on your device.

iCloud

If you use the iCloud sharing feature (available with SimpleTimer Pro), event data is shared through Apple's CloudKit service. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy and is only shared with users you explicitly invite. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Calendar Access

The App may request access to your calendar to import events. Calendar data is only read locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external server.

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to alert you when timers trigger. No notification data is sent to external servers.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Analytics

The App does not include any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
